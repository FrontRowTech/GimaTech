Overview

GIMA Technologies Consulting delivers high-quality civil engineering drawings and technical documentation to support infrastructure planning, approvals, and construction. Our services cover pipeline systems, chambers, crossings, and hydraulic designs required for compliant and efficient project execution.

What This Service Includes

Main Pipeline Drawings

Detailed design drawings for bulk and reticulation pipelines supporting water and sewer infrastructure.

Long & Cross Sections

Engineering long section and cross section drawings for accurate construction and approval purposes.

Jacking & Open Cut Drawings

Design documentation for trenchless (jacking) and open cut construction methods.

River & Stream Crossings

Engineering designs for river and stream crossings ensuring environmental and structural compliance.

Hydraulic Pressure Drawings

Hydraulic analysis and pressure drawings for pipeline systems and networks.

Valve & Chamber Details

Chamber detail arrangements including air valves, scour valves, sluice valves, and butterfly valves.

Pipe Specials Arrangement

Detailed drawings for bends, reducers, tees, and other pipe special components.

Meter & Precast Chambers

Meter connection chamber details and precast concrete chamber design drawings.

Schematic & Locality Plans

Schematic layout plans and locality plans for project presentation and approvals.
