About GIMA Technologies

GIMA Technologies Consulting is a proudly South African, B-BBEE Level 1 engineering and technology solutions provider. We specialise in wayleave management , fibre-optic design, civil engineering support, telecommunications infrastructure, and technical consultancy for both public and private sector projects.

Our team brings together advanced engineering expertise, hands-on field experience, and modern technology to deliver reliable, scalable, and compliant infrastructure solutions. We partner closely with clients to ensure projects are executed with precision, efficiency, and long-term sustainability.
