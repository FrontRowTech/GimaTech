Overview

Fibre and telecommunications infrastructure form the backbone of modern connectivity. GIMA Technologies Consulting delivers reliable, scalable, and compliant fibre network solutions for public and private sector clients — ensuring efficient rollout and long-term network performance.

What This Service Includes

Fibre Network Planning

Network route planning, feasibility studies, and infrastructure optimisation.

Fibre Design & Drawings

Detailed fibre layouts, splicing plans, and construction drawings aligned with industry standards.

Telecommunications Infrastructure

Support for ducts, poles, chambers, towers, and communication facilities.

Wayleave & Permit Coordination

Integration with wayleave and permitting processes to enable smooth deployment.

Construction Support

Technical oversight, as-built verification, and quality control during rollout.

Handover & Documentation

Final network documentation, GIS integration, and readiness for operations.
